This website show cases the second most commonly spoken languages in London boroughs. It is based on the data collected from the 2011 census. Every 10 years the census collects information about the population; from 1801 (except in 1941) and the latest census in England and Wales took place on 27 March 2011. The presence of each ethnicity is noticed as you walk down the streets of these boroughs from the shops to the local people.
